Hello, I'm Ahmed
Research And Development Engineer
Ahmed Mahgoub is a Research and Development Engineer with expertise in mechanical design, thermal systems, and product development across multiple industries. Currently, he works at Elsewedy Electric, a multinational leader in energy and infrastructure solutions, where he focuses on busway system optimization, compliance testing, and material selection. Concurrently, he contributes to Carrier Midea Egypt, specializing in HVAC system design, circuit simulation, and cross-functional collaboration with global teams. Previously, he played a crucial role in military engineering projects as a Mechanical Design Engineer, managing design reviews and project execution. His freelance experience spans robotics and automation, working on projects like the Humanoid Shams Robot, smart parking solutions, and MEP systems in Egypt and KSA. With a passion for education, Ahmed has also served as a private instructor, teaching courses in stress analysis, mechanics, and electronics. His diverse expertise in R&D, simulation, and design makes him a valuable asset in product innovation, compliance testing, and engineering solutions...
